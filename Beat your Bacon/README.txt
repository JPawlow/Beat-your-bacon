Game "Beat your bacon!"

In the game, player becomes funny, little burger, wanting to climb as high as possible. Higher you get, more points you earn, it's simple. You can control the burger with right/left arrows and spacebar to jump. 

Game was written as a students project, with JavaScript library - Phaser.
Authors: Jakub Pawlowski, Weronika Wolny, Weronika Golaszczyk
